Lorna Jane Mobile Application and Website Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Lorna Jane PTY LTD. (“Lorna Jane”) respects your privacy. We are particularly sensitive to privacy issues on the Internet and digital applications and therefore we provide this Privacy Policy so you know about our online information practices and understand how we treat the information we receive about you from the Internet and through our digital applications.

The Information We Collect

In general, you can use the application and other Lorna Jane owned digital properties without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We are, however, able to determine your IP address. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our website, to measure the use of our website and to improve the content of our website. In short we use your IP to gather aggregate demographic information.

There are a few places where we give you an opportunity to provide us your personal information. This refers to information that lets us know the specifics of who you are. When you engage in certain activities with the application, we may collect personal information to enhance the user experience.

When you use the mobile application, we may collect your Name, Facebook ID, Date Of Birth, Gender, Weight, & Height and other personal information. Providing this data is optional.

Sharing data

Users who accept the application via Facebook connect have the option to share their activity. These activities will post on the users Facebook Timeline with their name, their distance, their activity type & duration/time. Connecting the application with Facebook is optional.

Activity data captured

When using the Lorna Jane mobile application we will record certain information about your Run Walk and Cycle activities performed. This information will be recorded from all application users not just Facebook connected users. However Facebook connected users activities will be logged against their Facebook user ID & name.

Activity data includes:

  • Date of Activity: When the activity occurred
  • Type of Activity: Walking, Running & Cycling
  • Total Activity Time:
  • Distance Traveled
  • Calories Burned
  • Maximum Speed
  • Average Speed
  • Location Data related to these activities using GPS.

Support the Cause

A user may also sign up to “Support The Cause” via the Lorna Jane sponsored sites www.nowmoveit.com and www.nowmoveit.com.au. For the purposes of this initiative we will collect a users name, email address and mobile number. The user may also chose to opt in to receive future marketing communication from Lorna Jane. The user must agree to the terms of the privacy policy to continue.

Track the Movement

A map showing the location and type of application users’ activities (run/walk/cycle) will be displayed on http://nowmoveit.com/activity. The mobile application connects with this website to display application users activities (run/walk/cycle) as noted above. Users are not required to provide their personal information on this website and can chose to provide their information as an anonymous user.

If you choose to share any personal information with us, you will see that the amount and type of personal information we request depend on how you use the application. It is completely optional for you to engage in these activities. If you elect to engage in these activities, however, we may ask that you provide us personal information, such as your first and last name, mailing address (including postal code), email address, telephone and facsimile numbers, and other personal identifying information.

Depending upon the activity, some of the information we ask you to provide is identified as mandatory and some as voluntary. If you do not provide the mandatory data with respect to a particular activity, you will not be able to engage in that activity.

We may use your personal information to deliver information to you and to contact you regarding administrative notices. We may share this information with Lorna Jane affiliates or other business partners to support your customer relationship with the company. We will not give or sell this information to any other company for its use in marketing or solicitation.

Children's Privacy Policy

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA") requires that we inform parents and legal guardians about how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from children under 13 years of age, and that we obtain the consent of parents and guardians in order for children under 13 years of age to use certain features of our websites.  Lorna Jane does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

We may amend our Children's Privacy Policy at any time. We will provide parents notice by email or posting on our website of any material changes in the way we intend to collect, use, and/or share a child's personal information. We will apply material changes in our Children's Privacy Policy only in conformance with applicable law, including any applicable provisions of COPPA that require parental consent.

By using this application you are agreeing to the terms of the Lorna Jane Privacy Policy.  We reserve the right to modify our privacy policy as business needs require. We will post such changes on our website.
