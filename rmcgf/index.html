Frequently Asked Questions

What are the Terms and Conditions of Entry?

You must read and accept the full Terms and Conditions of the WAIVER to enter the event.

What does the entry fee include?

  • Food & drink at the rest stops along the way
  • Full route support marshals, signage, police, mechanics, medical, timing, & photography
  • Robbie McEwen Granfondo jersey (* All Road & MTB Fondos only, including all Kids4Kids events)

Are there any refunds?

There are no refunds for non starters or non finishers. When a participant Enters online or via the printed entry form, they accept the terms and conditions of entry. Please refer to Terms and Conditions in the WAIVER for full details.

Can I transfer my entry?

Entries cannot be transferred from one entrant to another.
