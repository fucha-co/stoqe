Our Programs

Recycling
mobilemuster.com.au

MobileMuster is the Australian mobile phone industry's official product stewardship program. It is the mobile phone industry's way of ensuring old mobile phone products are recycled instead of ending up as landfill.

More Info →

Lost & Stolen
amta.org.au/lost

The Australian mobile telecommunications industry offers free blocking for handsets reported lost or stolen. This is a powerful deterrent blocking phones across all networks, making them inoperable in Australia.

More Info →

Driving Safety
amta.org.au/ontheroad

Keep Your Eyes on the Road is the Australian mobile phone industry's initiative to encourage safe, responsible and legal mobile phone use while driving.

More Info →

Health & Safety
amta.org.au/health

On behalf of its member companies AMTA promotes the safe and responsible use of mobile telecommunications in Australia.

Mobile Tips
amta.org.au/mobile
